Creamy Strawberry Smoothie

Enjoy this thick smoothie with fine texture

Drinkable strawberry smoothie

ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy like almond, soy, or oat milk)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or regular yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional for sweetness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • ce cubes (optional, for a thicker consistency)

Instructions

  1. Prepare a cup
  2. put all ingredients in blender
  3. Blend
  4. Eat the smoothie
